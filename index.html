About SankhyaSangam

SankhyaSangam: InACT 2.0 – The Humanitarian Hackathon is an exciting initiative by Symbiosis Statistical Institute designed to challenge and inspire budding data enthusiasts. This event provides a unique platform for students to apply their statistical and analytical skills to real-world social issues, driving impactful and data-driven solutions.

Through collaboration, problem-solving, and innovation, participants will get hands-on exposure to the power of data in addressing humanitarian challenges. SankhyaSangam is not just a competition—it is an opportunity to harness the potential of statistics for social good and make a meaningful difference.

VIPLAV - Same Blood, New Veins

VIPLAV means "revolution" in Sanskrit. Revolution represents a change; it is a passionate act of hope to bring about a positive change.
It signifies the spirit of "same blood, new veins," which characterizes the human urge to evolve and improve without losing the qualities that define us as human beings and remain connected to our roots.


This event is inclined towards the future of Science, Technology, and Innovation : Impact on Education, Skills, and Work, with the objective to acknowledge the significance of statistics, science, and technology by expanding horizons for analytical, problem-solving, and innovative thinking to spread awareness about the importance of scientific applications in daily life.
